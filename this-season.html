COMBUSTIBLE LEMONS

The Combustible Lemons have been tasked with saving FIRST City from an incoming asteroid. Along with our trusty droids, we must ally with other FIRST teams in order to collect and score Power Cells to ensure our Shield Generator gives the city maximum protection. Wish us luck!

Get more information here: www.firstinspires.org/robotics/frc/game-and-season

Hatboro- Horsham District Competition 

The team had technical difficulties during the first quarter of the Hatboro-Horsham competition, which landed us the 32nd rank out of 36 teams. However, we ironed out our issues with the help of our mentors and the support of our sponsors. The team's enthusiasm in the stands never wavered and we went into the finals. In cooperation with our alliance partners, we became the second-place finalist and are advancing to the next competition.
