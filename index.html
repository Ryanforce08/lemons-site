COMBUSTIBLE LEMONS

FRC TEAM 5113

"When life gives you lemons, don't just make lemonade"

The Combustible Lemons are a 12-year robotics team, from Moorestown High School in Moorestown, New Jersey. The team was started in 2013 through the efforts of two parents and the support of teacher mentors. The team is supported by a strong parent booster organization. The Combustible Lemons emphasize the merits of coopertition and gracious professionalism. They are always interested in helping others, as well as building relationships with other teams. The Lemons are known for their goofy and fun-loving attitude, and emphasize fun and a cooperative attitude as their overall goal.
